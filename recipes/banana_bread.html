Banana Bread

Delicious, dense, and sweet banana based pastry

Banana bread is one of those dishes that if you grew up pretty much anywhere in the US, you probably have had it I feel like. It's a small but dense sweet bread made with ripe or mashed bananas, sugars, butters, and other ingredients depending on which mother you ask about it. This one is a personal favorite of my family we got from online with some modifications.

Ingredients

  • Bananas, of course.I typically use 1 whole and one half overripened ones.
  • 1/2 cup of Butter, preferably softened butter
  • 2 Eggs
  • Brown sugar, usually 1/2 a cup to a full cup
  • Greek yogurt, about 1/2 a cup to keep the bread moist and offset the sweetness slightly.
  • We sometimes add about 3/4 cup of chopped nuts to the recipe.
  • A pinch of salt to enhance flavor

Instructions

  1. Combine all the dry ingredients in a bowl
  2. Beat butter and brown sugar in a second bowl, then add eggs and banana
  3. Combine wet and dry mixtures
  4. Pour batter into a loaf pan and bake in a preheated oven at 350 degrees F for about an hour. Check to make sure bread is ready by sticking a toothpick in the center, make sure it comes out clean.
